3rd
Class Seats

Armchairs of the 3rd class, corresponding to GOST 34013-2016 "Passenger seat of multi-unit rolling stock and passenger cars of locomotive traction. General technical conditions ", have the widest application for the implementation of passenger transportation.

One-, two- and three-seater blocks of seats can be installed in locomotive-hauled passenger cars, multi-unit rolling stock, rail buses, motris and other types of rolling stock that provide transportation of passengers on urban and suburban routes, as well as personnel of organizations to the place of work.

The width of the 3rd class armchair blocks allows installation in the car according to the "2 + 3" scheme, which significantly increases the number of seats.
